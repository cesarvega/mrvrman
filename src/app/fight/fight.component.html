THE VR FIGHT CLUB

The Concept

OVERVIEW

The VR Fight club is a fun workout experience application, players will immerse themselves in an environment where they will be able to fist fight, train and listen to nice music and enjoy the light show just like a real experience of a boxing arena with disco lights, sound effects, and music from professional DJ's.

Under Development

VR ARENA

We are working hard to get the feel of VJ lights that goes with the rythm of the music, smoke machines, laser lights and a DJ booth just like a real arena enviroment experience.

Early Mechanics

TRAINING CONCEPT

Since the early development of the app, we wanted to create an application that hits targets at the rhythm of the music and create workouts with different speed and boxing throws to create different grades of difficulty, later on, we decide to create an avatar to be the opponent fighting back to the player, this will create more excitement and another level of challenge.

let's go DJ

MUSIC & SOUND FX

We are from Miami Florida, and this is home and place for most of the popular DJ's in the world to perform their music. We follow the music and its latest tracks, inspired by DJ's like Chus & Ceballos, Oscar L, Eric Murillo, we try to mix and master music that sounds like them in order to achieve a music experience that will give lost of motivations and entertainment to the player to work out intensively.

What we do the best!

STORY TELLING

From character design to game development design, we write the VR experiences as we tell a story, we focus in place actors and create scripts and animations for the actors to make the most VR immersive experience you can have.
